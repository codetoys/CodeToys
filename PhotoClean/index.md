#This app can quickly delete unnecessary photos and videos.

#Tips:
##1. Never delete favorite, never show favorite.
##2. If there isn't much storage space，Do Not delete too many photos.
##3. The photos deleted will be in Recently deleted in Photos，you MUST go to the Photos app to delete it completely, or recover them.
##3. Please do not clean the photos again before Recently deleted is empty.

#Important:
##If Recently deleted has too many photos, may be you can't "Delete all" of "Recover all".
This is not very certain, but it is not recommended that you take the risk.
